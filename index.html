Donald Trump

45th President of the United States

Here's a time line of Donald Trump's life:

  • 1946 - Born in Queens, New York City
  • 1959 - Enrolled in the New York Military Academy, a private boarding school
  • 1964 - Enrolled at Fordham University
  • 1966 - Transferred to the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, he also worked at the family business, Elizabeth Trump & Son
  • 1968 - Graduating with a B.S. in economics and starting career at real estate development company, E. Trump & Son
  • 1977 - Married Czech model Ivana Zelníčková
  • 1978 - Started the construction projects in Manhattan
  • 1983 - Completed development of Trump Tower
  • 1987 - With assocation of Tony Schwartz published the book "Trump: The Art of the Deal "
  • 1991 - First time when businesses of his have declared bankruptcy
  • 1993 - Married actress Marla Maples
  • 1999 - First attempt to became President of the United States
  • 2005 - Married Slovenian model Melania Knauss
  • 2015 - Announced his candidacy for President of the United States
  • 2016 - Was elected 45th President of the United States
  • 2017 - Declared a slogan "Keep America Great" for his reelection campaign 2020
